HIMEJI CITY

Food & Sake Tasting Event

Date: Wednesday, October 23, 2024
Venue: Sake +

Experience the culinary treasures of
Hyogo Prefecture's Himeji City, renowned for their rich bounty from
the Sea of Japan, Seto Inland Sea, and the Pacific Ocean.

The special event showcase three esteemed sake breweries,
premium seafood products, traditional Japanese sweets,
and the region's celebrated Ibonoito dried noodles.
